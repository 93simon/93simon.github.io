Fathers Day gift guide to Santana Row

There is less than a month until Father’s Day. This means it is time to think about what to get Dad, now! Thankfully, Santana Row has a large selection of clothing, accessories, sports gear, and more for Dad this Father’s Day. Love that!

Before presenting Dad with his fabulous Father’s Day gift from Santana Row, why not treat him to a little pampering; Dad-style on the Row! You can schedule a test drive appointment at Tesla on Father’s Day so that Dad can check out the new electric SUV! He will love it.

After Dad test drive’s the hot new Tesla SUV, why not treat him to a fabulous meal at The Counter, Rosie McCann’s, Maggiano’s, or LB Steak. If Dad is vegetarian, Veggie Grill is a great place to go!

Now that Dad has driven a fabulous, electric SUV, and he has enjoyed a yummy meal, it is time to present him with his Father’s Day gift. Now what to give him. . . Read on for my Father’s Day gift picks from Santana Row!
