Valentine’s Day gift guide to Santana Row

Valentine’s Day is almost here! Can you believe how fast the time goes? This means it is time to head over to Santana Row to find that perfect something for that special someone in your life :-)

I love Santana Row for Valentine’s Day because it is a one-stop-shop for everything Valentine’s Day! In order to create the perfect, romantic evening, you can make reservations at one of Santana Row’s fabulous restaurants for you and your loved one. My favorite Valentine’s day restaurants are LB Steak, Citrus, and Sino.

At dinner, you will want to give the perfect gift to your special loved one; read on for fabulous gift ideas from Santana Row.

To complete the gift, you can also find the perfect Valentine’s Day card at The Paper Source; as well as Valentine’s Day gifts for children. Love that!

In addition to beautiful artwork, and home decor, Annie Glass carries fashionable jewelry from designers known for their unique style, and enviable pieces.
