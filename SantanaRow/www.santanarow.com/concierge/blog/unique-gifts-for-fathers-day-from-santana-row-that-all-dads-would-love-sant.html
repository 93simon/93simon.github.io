Unique Gifts for Father’s Day from Santana Row that all Dads would love – Santana Row Father’s Day G

Hello! Welcome back to Muse Meileena! Father’s Day is almost here and I’ve teamed up with Santana Row, the ultimate destination for style (and my absolute favorite place to shop), to bring youunique Father’s Day gifts that you can find on the Row! We often go gift shopping with a list of people we need to shop for, so I’ve shared gift ideas from different Santana Row shops for all the Father’s in your life! The best weekend to shop is this weekend! The newly opened Maker’s Market is having a lovely shopping event – so I’ll leave details after the gift guide so you can plan your shopping trip!

~ For your Dad ~ 

Something Stylish: Head to Franco Uomo and find him a unique tie that he’ll treasure! (Good for dad who have lots of ties and you don’t want to get one similar to something he already has!) 

Something Sharp: Does he love to cook? Could he use a new knife set? Head to Sur La Table and select the best of the best (at great prices!) for Dad. If not knives, lots of other culinary gadgets he’s sure to appreciate.

Something Simple: Head to Tommy Bahama for a wonderful selection of easy-breezy short-sleeved shirts that are perfect for summer. 

For your love ~ 

Something Stylish: Treat him to a custom fitting experience at Bonobos Guide Shop! Has he been needing a new suit? He’ll feel so special going to the fit shop to order a suit that fits perfectly, and that he’ll love wearing for years to come.  Or, is he needing a new pair of shoes? Head to none other than Donald J. Pliner and find him a pair of comfortable, stylish, and well-made shoes. You can even get him some wax and a brush to complete the gift. 

Something to carry-all: Head to Ted Baker – not only for more great clothing options, but also for accessories. Could go for a wallet, but why not go for a great carry-all bag? Thoughtful and long-lasting.Perfect for those who travel a lot, for work, with family, and can’t forget those weekend getaways!

Something Nostalgic: Maker’s Market at Santana Row is now open, featuring locally-made handcrafted goods and they are also having an awesome Maker’s Market in the Park Shopping Event this Saturday! Go and pick up something thoughtfully created for the one you love. Did he used to skateboard? Bring back memories with a handmade in San Francisco beautiful upcycled skateboard.

Or head to Urban Outfitters and get him a vintage inspired Record player and/or records from musicians he loves. 

Something to share: Why not get a new bed set for the two of you? You know Anthropologie’s got the best.

For other Fathers in your life ~ 

Something thoughtful:  Got a glasses-wearer? He’ll definitely appreciate all that is Warby Parker! You can get him a gift card so he can go try on and then order a great pair that he loves (prescription included!) OR you can just get him some awesome sunglasses. Winning all around.

Something monogrammed: Anthropologie‘s got some great mugs – grab some for the Father’s in your life, from friends to In-laws.

Something for the day-to-day: Technical Cashmere from Kit and Ace has elevated day-to-day basics with quality and comfort. Easy to choose a shirt or jacket for the men on your list, and they’ll love how soft it is and want to wear it everyday! 

Something fun: Men love socks. Especially funny socks. ‘Nuff said! Head to Urban Outfitters for more like this.

Still unsure? You can always treat him to a Santana Row gift card in any amount to be used at shops on the Row! Head to the concierge and they’ll take care of you!

Oh and not to forget – cards and sweets!

For cards: Head to Mai Do or to Paper Source!

For sweets: Stop by Sugarfina! You can create your own candy bento box and lots lots more! Ask about the Vice Collection Bento Box – it’s perfect for Father’s Day!

I hope you’ve enjoyed this gift guide and feel ready to shop this weekend! This weekend is the best time to shop and Maker’s Market is having an awesome party to celebrate their grand opening and for pre-Father’s Day shopping! I will be there and I hope to see you too! Join us for shopping local artisans work, Wine tasting from Scheid Vineyards, local musicians, and bites from Sino, Straits and Roots and Rye. Click here to RSVP and for all of the event info! Do let me know if you’re coming!

Stay tuned for my next post on Unique Ways to Celebrate Father’s Day at Santana Row! Thank you so much for reading!

Love always,

Muse Meileena
