Cleaning and Care



*DO NOT clean ANY surface, or part until it is FULLY cooled from heat exposure and in a well ventilated area.

Treat sticky, or affected tool surfaces with a non-abrasive fabric cloth, or pad soaked with Isopropyl alcohol and wipe the surfaces clean. You can also, briefly soak the affected tool in a container with Isopropyl alcohol and stir until the residue is removed. This should only take 30 seconds to 1 minute to work. Finally, dry and wipe clean with a non-abrasive fabric cloth, or pad before using again.

*Do NOT soak in alcohol, or expose to high heat for prolonged periods at a time for product longevity.
