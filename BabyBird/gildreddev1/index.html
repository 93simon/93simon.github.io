LEGACY

Since 1927 the Gildred family has been a part of the fabric of San Diego, investing in and supporting America’s Finest City and the region.

Four generations of Gildreds have demonstrated commitment to this community. The Gildred Companies began with the construction and operation of the landmark San Diego Fox Theater Building now Copley Symphony Hall and Symphony Towers.

Today the Gildred Companies is a diversified company with assets throughout Southern California including a portfolio of industrial and business parks, new developments in real estate and renewable power, as well as strategic investments in business opportunities that support San Diego’s economy.

The Gildred Companies takes pride in its long legacy spanning multiple generations with a reputation of integrity, providing competitive advantages to businesses and making a difference.

PROJECTS IN DEVELOPMENT

  • Ocotillo Wells Solar: This 50 Mega Watt solar project is located on 440 acres in East San Diego County. This project will provide enough clean renewable energy to power 20,000 homes.
  • Alpine Ridge: The Gildred Companies’ planned 310 acre residential project consists of 33 hilltop home sites with stunning views of San Diego’s east county.
  • Ramona Vines: In Ramona’s wine country, the company is developing a project in an area covered with oak trees and grape vines amidst rolling hills.
