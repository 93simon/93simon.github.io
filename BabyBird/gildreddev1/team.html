Our Team

George Gildred

Director of Acquisitions and Dispositions

George has provided leadership and guidance at the Gildred Companies for over fifty years. Active in real estate investment in the area for many decades, George is committed to his community. He was recognized as one of San Diego’s distinguished leaders when he received the Mr. San Diego Award, an award which his father, Philip Gildred Sr. received in 1974 for service to the community. He served as Chair of the San Diego Convention and Visitors Bureau, Hubbs Sea World Research Institute, the University of California San Diego Development Council and the Committee to build the University of California San Diego Cancer Center.

George is past Chairman and President of San Diego Zoo Global and he led the first Zoo delegation to China in 1979. He has also provided leadership in the community as the Co-Chairman of the Citizens Committee to build the San Diego Stadium (formerly Qualcomm Stadium) and is a Past President of the Rotary Club of San Diego. He serves as Honorary Consul of the Republic of Chile in San Diego. Following graduation from Stanford University, George served as an officer in the United States Navy from 1956 to 1960.

Phil Gildred

Director of Acquisitions and Dispositions

Phil Gildred is the Gildred Companies’ most tenured employee providing leadership to the company since 1961. The Gildred Companies has a legacy of developing commercial real estate throughout the San Diego community, and one of giving back in the process. Phil shares his father’s vision of making a difference and has always been involved in San Diego business and philanthropy.

He is the Founding President of both the Sharp Hospital Foundation and the University Club Atop Symphony Towers. Phil is the past President of the Board of several San Diego organizations including the San Diego Chapter of Building Owners and Managers Association, Chairman of the Pacific (Southwest Regional Conference), the Executives Association of San Diego, Board of Directors Francis W. Parker School and the San Diego Automotive Museum.

He has a long list of philanthropic and civic organizations on whose boards he has served including the American Red Cross and the YMCA Camp Committee. He is a long term Board Member of the San Diego Historical Society and served as its Vice President.

Laura Barreiro

Director of Acquisitions and Dispositions

Laura Barreiro, CCIM, is Director of Acquisitions and Dispositions for the Gildred Companies. In her role she provides strategic planning, identification and analysis of opportunities and transaction negotiation. She is responsible for site selection, financial modeling and all stages of execution for acquisitions and dispositions. Laura started with Gildred in February 2011 and has experience across many aspects of the company’s operations. Her prior role at Gildred was Manager of Leasing, overseeing leasing for the portfolio in excess of one million square feet.

Laura enjoys spending time with family and friends when she is not at work, seeking out new experiences close to home in one of the many beautiful spots to see around San Diego County.

Becky Blair

Accounts Receivable Accountant

Becky Blair has been part of the Gildred Companies team since 1995. As the Accounts Receivable Accountant she is responsible for lease documents, cash receipts and collections as well as monthly statements.

She is proud of past accomplishments and looks forward to growth and new developments in the future. Before Gildred, she worked with Progress Management, Conrad Prebys and the McMillin Companies.

When she’s not at work, Becky enjoys Roadtreking, walking and reading.

Korinne DeJesus

Accounts Payable Specialist

Korinne DeJesus provides support to the company as the Accounts Payable Specialist. She maintains all vendor accounts, manages payments and provides support to property managers and vendors. Korinne is very serious about her work at Gildred, but she likes approaching it with a lighthearted spin. She enjoys bringing cheer and connection to the workplace. She has been with the team since 2012.

Korinne has worked in the accounting field since 2003 and is highly experienced with the day in and day out of accounts payable. She is happy to bring that expertise to the team at Gildred.

While not at work Korinne enjoys spending time with family.

Sofia DeLaCruz

Accounting Manager

Sofia DeLaCruz is Accounting Manager for the Gildred Companies. She brings 20 years of property accounting expertise including overseeing cash flow, CAM reconciliations, fixed assets and preparing financial statements. Sofia leads the Gildred accounting team.

Sofia recently earned her Professional Certificate in Accounting through the UCSD Extension program. She is also a California Tax Education Council (CTEC) Registered Tax Preparer.

When she is not managing the accounting department you can catch her hiking the trails of San Diego, visiting one of the area’s many great restaurants, enjoying theater and spending time with family. Sofia’s daughter Ashley is also part of the Gildred team.
